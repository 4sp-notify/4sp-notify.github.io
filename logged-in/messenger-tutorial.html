Powered by Firebase: This messenger is now internet-based. You can message anyone who has the app, regardless of their WiFi network.

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Go to Link" button below to open the Google Drive page containing the app files.
  2. On the Google Drive page, click the download icon in the top-right corner to save the .zip file to your Chromebook.
  3. Open the "Files" app on your Chromebook and go to your "Downloads" folder.
  4. Double-click the .zip file you just downloaded. This will mount it and show its contents.
  5. To start the app, double-click the messenger.html file.
  6. The app will give you a 8-character long ID. Share this ID with your friends so they can add you as a contact!
