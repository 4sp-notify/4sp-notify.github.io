What is a Replicated Request?

To improve database efficiency, this system reuses the "slots" of requests that are over 21 days old.

Your submission may be placed into one of these recycled slots. This is a background process that you can see, but it is invisible to other users and has no effect on your request's visibility, voting, or potential implementation.

This process helps keep the platform fast and free.
