Streamline Classroom Communication

Attentio provides a simple, direct way for students to make requests and for teachers to manage them. Accept, deny, or add comments with ease.

How It Works

Our platform is built for simplicity. Students submit a request through their dashboard. Teachers receive a notification and can respond instantly, keeping everyone on the same page without the clutter of email.

Our Mission

We aim to create focused and efficient communication tools for the modern classroom, reducing administrative friction and allowing more time for learning.
